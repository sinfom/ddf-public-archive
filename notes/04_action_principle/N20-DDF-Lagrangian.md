# DDF Lagrangian

The projection framework admits an action formulation.

$$
\mathcal{L}
=
(L\psi \cdot L\psi)
-
(\psi \cdot M\psi)
$$

Variation yields

$$
(L^\dagger L)\psi = M\psi
$$

---

## Interpretation

This equation acts as the master relation from which:

- Schrödinger equation
- Maxwell equations
- Einstein equations
- wave equations
- diffusion equations

emerge as restricted limits.
