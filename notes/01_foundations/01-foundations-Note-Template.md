# 01_foundations — Note Template

This is the standard structure for foundations‑level notes in `01_foundations`.

---

# Title

Format:

- `F# — Descriptive Title` for foundational notes, or  
- `N‑X# — Descriptive Title` for meta/progress notes (e.g. N‑C0).

---

## Purpose

Briefly state the goal of the note within the foundations layer:

- What aspect of projection, admissibility, sectors, generator, or constants is being clarified?  
- How does it support later operator‑level notes?

---

## Definitions

List all symbols, operators, and key terms used in this note.

- Reference `01_foundations/ddf_notation.md` where possible.  
- Introduce any new symbol here and then add it to the local notation file.

---

## Core Statement / Theorem

State the main structural statement or conceptual principle.

- For purely conceptual notes, this may be a clear structural assertion rather than a formal theorem.  
- For meta notes (like N‑C0), this can be a concise status statement.

---

## Derivation / Explanation

Include all reasoning, equations, and derivation steps.

- Do not delete or compress existing derivations when refactoring older notes into this structure.  
- Alternative derivations should be clearly labelled within this section.

---

## Consequences

List direct implications within the foundations layer, and how this note supports:

- spectral structure,  
- sector structure,  
- gauge redundancy,  
- structural constants,  
- links to operator‑level notes.

---

## Dependencies

List prerequisites by label and title, for example:

- `Requires F1 — Harmonic Projection`  
- `Requires F2 — Projection Constraints`

---

## Next Notes

List notes that logically follow, for example:

- `Leads to F3 — Spectral Selection`  
- `Leads to F6 — Fermionic and Bosonic Sectors`

---

## Notes / Working Material

Preserve:

- historical versions,  
- exploratory calculations,  
- partial arguments,  
- TODOs and open questions.

Never delete material; move anything non‑central here when restructuring.
