# Start Here

The Dual Domain Framework begins with the proposal that observable physical structure arises through a projection from a deeper generative domain into the observable universe.

The central question is:

> What structures must survive projection if the observable domain is to remain finite, coherent, stable, and physically meaningful?

The archive develops this question through a chain of notes and papers covering projection ontology, admissibility, operator emergence, propagation rigidity, spin structure, quantum structure, curvature, gravity limits, and number-theoretic extensions.
