# Status of the Framework

DDF is a speculative structural research programme. It is not presented as an experimentally confirmed physical theory and not as a completed Standard-Model-level unification.

At its current stage, the framework aims to organise a coherent derivation chain from projection admissibility toward:

- finite propagation
- Lorentzian kinematics
- Dirac and spin structure
- phase-space and quantum structure
- curvature response
- a spectral-action route toward an Einstein-like gravity limit

Important open problems remain, including:

- derivation of the full Standard Model gauge group
- matter spectrum and representation structure
- coupling running and renormalisation-group behaviour
- numerical determination of physical constants
- internal identification of the relevant spectral cutoff scales

Notes in this archive should therefore be read as a mixture of conceptual framing, formal derivation attempts, conditional arguments, and open research programmes.
