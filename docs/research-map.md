## Repository Layers

- `notes/00_index/` - master indexes, notation, dependency maps, and global framework maps.
- `notes/01_foundations/` - projection ontology and foundational constraints.
- `notes/02_operator_notes/` - admissibility, propagation, wave/operator structure, Dirac factorisation, gauge structure, and constants.
- `notes/03_geometry/` - geometric and Selberg-trace-related structures.
- `notes/04_action_principle/` - Lagrangian, action functional, and unified field equations.
- `notes/05_gravity_limit/` - curvature, Lichnerowicz identity, spectral action, and gravity limit.
- `notes/06_quantum_structure/` - projection degeneracy, entanglement, operator algebras, and quantum extensions.
- `notes/07_Number_Theory/` - arithmetic and spectral-number-theory interfaces.
- `notes/09_progress_numeric_projection/` - numerical projection work.
- `notes/10_notes_Papers/` - working paper drafts and related notes.
