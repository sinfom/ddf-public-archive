The foundations layer introduces the projection ontology and the admissibility constraints that define the framework.

Core themes include:

- the relation between a generative domain and the observable universe
- finite observability as a constraint on projection
- spectral selection and admissible modes
- emergence of particle discreteness
- structural roles of physical constants
- gauge redundancy from projection

The full foundation notes are in `notes/01_foundations/` in the repository.
